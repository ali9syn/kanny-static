  • Dates: 15 and 16 March
  • Time: 9:30am – 5pm
  • Book by: Four weeks before the course starts

    • A well-fitting pattern is an essential part of footwear design, helping to create well- fitting uppers and distinctive bespoke shoes.

    On this practical 2-day course you will learn the various skills required to make paper patterns for a Loafer (a slip-on) shoe by hand.

    Course description

    Working either on your own lasts or ours, you will make a full set of patterns (including linings) for a classic Loafer shoe. These and the comprehensive notes supplied in class will provide a strong foundation from which to create your own Loafer footwear patterns in the future.

    The course is taught by Fiona Campbell, MA who teaches the pattern maker’s techniques through demonstration, trial and guidance including:

    • Familiarisation with the last
    • Taping and designing onto the last
    • Making a paper mean forme
    • Learning about edge treatments and seam allowances
    • Making a standard from the last
    • Creating the pattern pieces from the standard

    Materials

    All the materials needed for the course are provided.

    • A well-fitting pattern is an essential part of footwear design, helping to create well-fitting uppers and distinctive bespoke shoes.

    This practical and intensive course demonstrates the aspects of footwear pattern cutting that make a shoe upper design unique and the various skills required to make paper patterns by hand.

    Course description

    Working either on your own lasts or ours, you will make a full set of patterns (including linings) for a loafer, a classic Oxford shoe and a Derby shoe. These and the comprehensive notes supplied in class will provide a strong foundation from which to create your own footwear patterns in the future.

    The course is taught by Fiona Campbell, MA who teaches the pattern maker’s techniques through demonstration, trial and guidance including:

    • Familiarisation with the last
    • Taping and designing onto the last
    • Making a paper mean forme
    • Learning about edge treatments and seam allowances
    • Making a standard from the last
    • Creating the pattern pieces from the standard

    We understand that students will work at different paces and for those who have time at the end of the course, there will the opportunity to customise their patterns under the tutor’s guidance.

    Materials

    All the materials needed for the course are provided.

    Special offer

    The Footwear Pattern Making courses are timed to dovetail with our renowned 12-day Shoe Making courses, providing the opportunity for aspiring shoemakers across the world to learn the essentials of these highly skilled disciplines. There is a special discount if you book both courses together.
