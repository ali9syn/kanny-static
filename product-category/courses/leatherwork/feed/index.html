    • There’s no greater feeling than the satisfaction of making something yourself and our hand stitched leather courses offer the perfect opportunity; with short evening, half day and day courses to choose from. On this short course you will learn to make a beautiful, Italian leather card case, perfectly sized for travel, business or bank cards.

    Course description

    The course is taught by leather craftsman Frankey Pinnock (link to biog) formerly of Bill Amberg Studio and now a bag maker for Simpsons. Through demonstration, trial and guidance she will teach you:
    • How to prepare and finish the leather edges
    • How to cement the layers of leather together
    • How to punch the stitch holes and to set your work in a clam
    • How to use an awl and stitching needles
    • How to saddle stitch and to finish your work
    We hope that this practical course and the comprehensive notes supplied in class will set you up for saddle stitching in the future.
